Established in 1966 NUAC aims to offer varied recreational angling opportunities in and around Norfolk to members of all abilities.

Whether you wish to fish for specimen carp or pike, trot for roach and chub, bag up on Broads bream or while away a few hours beside a lake then NUAC has something to offer you.

Membership is by application only and at the discretion of the committee

As of June 2022 the club is no longer accepting new members. Please check back here in June 2023 for an update.
